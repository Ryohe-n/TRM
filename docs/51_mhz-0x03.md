##### 4.1.1.4.3 UFS
- Automotive customers have requested the UFS controller and storage as the secondary boot
media, in place of eMMC. UFS devices support higher read and write speeds. This could potentially reduce flash times and boot KPIs considerably for automotive customers. So UFS boot for automotive, with safety certification, is supported. In the SoC, there is no requirement for 1.8V I/O sideband signal level on UFS. Sideband signal level 1.2V is supported.
