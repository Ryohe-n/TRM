# 22 :16 0x0 TX_FIFO_TRIG: Slave Transmit FIFO trigger level. 0000000 = 1 word, trigger is asserted when at least one word empty in the FIFO. 0000001 = 2 words, trigger is asserted when at least two words empty in the FIFO..... 1111111 = 128 words, trigger is asserted when 128 words empty in the FIFO.
